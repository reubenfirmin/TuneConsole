1 · Capture your sign-in

  1. Open music.youtube.com signed in, then open DevTools (F12) → Network.
  2. Click any playlist (e.g. in the left sidebar) — that fires a browse request. Type browse in the filter and click that browse request row.
  3. Right-click it → Copy → Copy as cURL (Windows: Copy as cURL (bash)), then paste below.
