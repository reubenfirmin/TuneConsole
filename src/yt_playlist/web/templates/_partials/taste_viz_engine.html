Playlist influence permanent

The recommender doesn't blur all your music into one average. It treats each playlist as a distinct sound and blends them, weighting each by how much you actually listen to it. So your heavy-rotation playlists steer your picks, while a rarely-played one (the road-trip-with-Dad playlist) barely counts and can't drag recommendations off-taste. Each bar is a playlist's share of that blend; hover for its dominant genre.
