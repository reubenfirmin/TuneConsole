Genre families For You only

Each knob multiplies the taste-match score of every track in that family when ranking the For You feed (and nothing else): 1.0 = neutral, 0 mutes the family (sinks to the bottom), 2 doubles its scores (floats to the top). The % is that family's share of your listening, shown for context only; it does not set the knob. You set these by hand, but the Learning section below can also nudge them on its own over time.
